About Snoopy X

I'm Joy Mokadi, also known as Snoopy X, the Creepy Threader. I'm from Mamelodi East, Pretoria, Gauteng, South Africa. A curious soul with a passion for uncovering the bizarre and the unexplained. My journey into the world of creepy facts and mysteries started when I was a child, fascinated by the unknown and strange occurrences around the world.

I am a seeker of all things eerie, from haunted places to unsolved mysteries. I’ve made it my mission to bring you some of the most spine-chilling facts out there, and I share them with you in a way that’ll make you think twice before you go to sleep tonight!

In addition to creepy facts, I also delve into fascinating topics like the science of the unknown, the psychology of fear, and strange phenomena that will make your skin crawl. My goal is to keep you on the edge of your seat with every new thread I share.
